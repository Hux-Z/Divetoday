02 · First Responder

Your job is not to be a doctor 第一反应员不是医生

Protect

Check scene safety first先检查现场安全

Do not rush into danger. Look, listen, and think before acting.不要冲进危险。行动前先观察、聆听、思考。

Activate

Call EMS early尽早启动 EMS

Give bystanders clear jobs: call emergency services, bring the AED, guide EMS.给旁观者明确任务：打急救电话、拿 AED、引导急救人员。

Care

Provide care within training在训练范围内照护

Continue until relieved, unsafe, exhausted, or the patient recovers.持续照护，直到有人接替、现场不安全、自己耗尽，或患者恢复。

04 · Primary Assessment

First check: life threats 第一轮检查：危及生命的问题

Primary means first and most important. Assessment means evaluation.Primary 是第一、最重要。Assessment 是评估。

We are not diagnosing everything. We are asking: safe scene, responsive patient, normal breathing, need for CPR/AED or urgent care.我们不是诊断所有问题，而是在问：现场安全吗？患者有反应吗？呼吸正常吗？是否需要 CPR/AED 或紧急照护？

Danger: check hazards and protect yourself.危险：检查风险，先保护自己。

Response: tap, speak, and use the responder statement.反应：轻拍、呼喊，并使用第一反应员声明。

Send: call EMS and send for AED.呼叫：启动 EMS，并派人取 AED。

Airway and Breathing: gasping is not normal breathing.气道与呼吸：濒死喘息不是正常呼吸。

CPR/AED: act immediately when unresponsive and not breathing normally.CPR/AED：无反应且无正常呼吸时立刻行动。

05 · AB-CABS

A memory word for the care pathway 用一个记忆词串起照护路径

A/B

Airway and breathing气道与呼吸

Is the airway open? Is the patient breathing normally?气道是否打开？患者呼吸是否正常？

CAB

CPR cycleCPR 循环

Chest compressions, airway, breaths. CPR takes priority.胸外按压、打开气道、人工呼吸。CPR 优先。

S

Serious threats严重威胁

Manage serious bleeding, shock, and spinal concerns.处理严重出血、休克和脊柱疑虑。

Check → Decide → Act → Recheck → Continue检查 → 判断 → 行动 → 再检查 → 持续照护

The Cycle of Care means you continue moving through priorities until professional help arrives, the AED is ready, you are relieved, the scene becomes unsafe, or the patient recovers.照护循环的意思是：你持续按照优先顺序行动，直到专业救援到达、AED 准备好、有人接替、现场不安全，或患者恢复。

06 · Drowning Focus

Water emergencies are breathing emergencies 水中事故首先是呼吸问题

Call First

Most adult cardiac emergencies大多数成人心脏类紧急情况

Activate EMS first, then provide care. Use a phone on speaker if alone.先启动 EMS，再提供照护。如果独自一人，用手机免提。

Care First

Submersion, drowning, children, infants浸没、溺水、儿童、婴儿

Provide a short period of CPR, especially rescue breaths, then call EMS and retrieve AED if close by.先进行一小段 CPR，特别重视人工呼吸，然后呼叫 EMS，并在附近有 AED 时取得 AED。

Once safe and out of the water, breathing is the priority.一旦安全并离开水面，呼吸就是优先事项。

If they are unresponsive and not breathing normally, we need rescue breaths and CPR, and we need EMS as early as possible.如果对方没有反应且没有正常呼吸，我们需要人工呼吸和 CPR，也要尽早启动 EMS。

07 · CPR / AED

Demonstrate the sequence, not perfection 演示顺序，不追求完美

CPR practice scriptCPR 练习讲稿

Check the scene. Is it safe?检查现场。安全吗？

Tap and shout: Are you OK?轻拍并呼喊：你还好吗？

Call EMS. Bring the AED.呼叫 EMS。拿 AED。

Open airway, check breathing, start CPR if needed.打开气道，检查呼吸，必要时开始 CPR。

Push hard and fast. Minimize pauses.用力、快速按压。尽量减少停顿。

AED safety lineAED 安全线

Turn it on and follow the voice prompts.打开 AED，并跟随语音提示。

Do not touch during analysis or shock.分析或电击时不要接触患者。

Dry the chest pad area if the patient is wet.患者身上有水时，擦干贴片区域。

Resume CPR immediately when instructed.设备提示后，立即恢复 CPR。

08 · Pool Session

DSD: calm, close, controlled DSD：冷静、靠近、可控

Rule 01

Breathe continuously持续呼吸

Never hold your breath. Stop, breathe, look at me, signal.绝不憋气。停下、呼吸、看教练、打手势。

Rule 02

Equalize early尽早平衡耳压

Do not force it. Signal and go shallower if needed.不要硬顶。必要时打手势并回浅。

Rule 03

Stay close靠近教练

This is a supervised experience, not independent diving.这是受监督体验，不是独立潜水。

Rule 04

Signal clearly清楚打手势

OK, problem, up, down, stop, slow, equalize.OK、有问题、上升、下潜、停止、慢一点、平衡耳压。

You are allowed to pause at any time.你可以随时暂停。

A pause is not a failure. Signal me, breathe slowly, and we solve one thing at a time.暂停不是失败。给我手势，慢慢呼吸，我们一次解决一个问题。

Regulator breathing while standing or kneeling.站立或跪姿用调节器呼吸。

Face in water breathing and equalization practice.脸入水后持续呼吸，并练习平衡耳压。

Regulator clearing and recovery.二级头排水和找回二级头。

Mask clearing as required.按要求完成面镜排水。

Short swim under direct instructor control.在教练直接控制下短距离游动。

09 · Debrief

One shared mindset 两件事，一个心态

Emergency response and scuba both rely on simple procedures, practice, and clear communication.紧急反应和水肺潜水都依赖简单流程、练习和清楚沟通。

In EFR, students learn how to respond instead of freeze. In DSD, they learn how to stay calm underwater: breathe continuously, equalize, signal, and stay with the instructor.在 EFR 中，学员学习如何回应，而不是僵住。在 DSD 中，他们学习如何在水下保持冷静：持续呼吸、平衡耳压、打手势，并靠近教练。
